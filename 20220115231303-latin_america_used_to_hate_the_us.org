In Caracas, Venezuela, Richard Nixon was once attacked ([[https://www.history.com/this-day-in-history/vice-president-nixon-is-attacked][1]], [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attack_on_Richard_Nixon's_motorcade][2]]). People in Venezuela were
pretty aware at the time (1958) of US dictatorial policies. For an example, the
US awarded Marcos Perez Jimenez (1954), the right-wing dictator, the =Legion of
Merit= medal [[https://qcostarica.com/the-history-and-hypocrisy-of-us-meddling-in-venezuela/][(3)]].

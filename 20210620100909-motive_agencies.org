#+title: Motive Agencies

According to [[file:20210620094256-kenneth_burke.org][Kenneth Burke]]'s [[file:20210620094340-dramatism.org][Dramatism]], there are five agencies,
- Agent
- Agency
- Scene
- Purpose
- Act
  Seen in, in a pentagram, in the [[file:20210620094516-dramatistic_pentad.org][Dramatistic Pentad.]] These, analyzed, make it possible to arrive at an Attitude - a Motive. Thereof, we can understand a [[file:20210620093607-grammar_of_motives.org][Grammar of Motives]] by carefully observing these [[file:20210620100909-motive_agencies.org][Motive Agencies]].
